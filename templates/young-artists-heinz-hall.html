Heinz Hall Performances

Honoring the Best of the Best of our 2025-2026 SSWPA Young Artists

These young artists were chosen by our judge to play in the lobby of Heinz Hall before Pittsburgh Symphony Orchestra concerts, mostly ones which feature a piano soloist. Out of the 2 full days of auditions, this group of students proved themselves to be the best that Pittsburgh has to offer. We hope that we'll see you at an upcoming PSO concert, where you'll not only get to hear world-renowned concert pianists, but also these local stars!

Membership

Become a Member of the Steinway Society

You are invited to join the Steinway Society of Western Pennsylvania! By becoming a member, you are eligible for free tickets to our entire Recital Series (4 recitals). All recitals include a "Meet the Artist" reception after the performance.

You will also be notified about Young Artist Honors Recitals and Master Classes.

Membership Levels

Individual

Family
Family Memberships include four tickets to each of the Artist Recitals of the series.

Donations above and beyond the membership fee are welcomed and encouraged. Any donations beyond the membership fee are fully tax deductible under section 501(c)(3) of the IRS guidelines for non-profit organizations.

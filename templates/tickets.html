No ticket purchase is required for Steinway Society members and CMU students (with ID).

Become a Member

Members are eligible for free tickets to our entire Recital Series (4 recitals). We have two forms of membership:

  • Individual Members ($75 per season) receive one admission to all Artist Recitals of the series.
  • Family Memberships ($125 per season) include four tickets to each of the Artist Recitals of the series.

Parking Information

Parking is free on Sundays on the following streets at Carnegie Mellon University: Frew Street, Tech Street, and Margaret Morrison Street.

The CMU Morewood lot (view on Google Maps) is also free and open to anyone on the weekends. A 5-10 minute walk to the Theatre, the Morewood Lot has its entrance on Morewood Avenue between Fifth and Forbes.

Audition Location

Eligibility for Young Artist Program

  • Pianists up to age 18, or through their senior year in High School (whichever is later).
  • Program must be memorized and 5-15 minutes in length (standard repertoire).
  • A SSWPA family membership of $150, plus $25 for each additional sibling young artist
  • Teacher membership (single: $75, family: $125)

Acceptance into the SSWPA Young Artists program is contingent upon receiving both the student's family and teacher membership; absolutely no exceptions.

Fill out the APPLICATION FORM and return by May 17th, 2025, to:
SSWPA, P.O.BOX 103, Wildwood PA 15091-0103

Include:

  • A check for the membership fee of $150 for one child, $175 for two children, $200 for three children, payable to SSWPA

Alternatively: You may also e-mail your application as a WORD DOCUMENT to: marinalupinacci1@gmail.com

Please choose a 3-hour window with a preferred audition time and day. We will try to meet your preference as closely as possible.

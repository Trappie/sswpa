2025-2026 Honors Recital Dates

SSWPA Young Artist honors recitals will be held on:

Sunday, December 9, 2025

Sunday, April 5, 2026

All of these recitals begin at 3:00 PM.

PYCO School of Music
15 Lake Dr, Suite 103, Wexford, PA 15090

Honors Recital Guidelines

  • Participation as a performer on the Honors Recitals is open to pianists on the Young Artist Roster.
  • Your program should not exceed 10 minutes.
  • All pieces must be memorized.
  • You should send your program information via Email 10-14 days prior to the recital date to: marinalupinacci1@gmail.com
  • Programs notes should include:
    • Title of the piece including movement names (if applicable) and with Op. number, if stated
    • Full name of composer with birth-death years
  • This is a "dress up" affair - please dress accordingly.
